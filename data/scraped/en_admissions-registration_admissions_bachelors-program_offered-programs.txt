Bridging conditions for holders of a diplomas who are registered in higher education institutions before 15/6/2023:
1- For health specialties except medicine, dentistry and pharmacy.
Have a diploma with an average of at least 60%
Pass the comprehensive exam with at least a ranking of “Passed”
The diploma specialization must be corresponding to the bachelor’s major
Obtain a certificate showcasing at least one year of practical experience in the field of specialization
Pass the interview and/or entrance exam held by the College Admission Committee
2- Engineering majors.
Have a high school diploma in the scientific branch
Completion of the intermediate diploma with a cumulative GPA of (75%) or higher
Pass the comprehensive applied exam with a very good grade (75%) effectively
The diploma specialization must be corresponding to the bachelor’s major
Pass the interview and/or entrance exam held by the College Admission Committee
The student at the university is given at least three remedial courses related to the specialization as the university deems appropriate, provided that he achieves in these courses a mark of (75%) or higher
3 – Other disciplines except law
Have a diploma with an average of at least 60%
Pass the comprehensive exam with at least a successful grade
The diploma specialization should be corresponding to the bachelor’s major
Obtain a certificate showcasing at least one year of practical experience in the field of specialization
Pass the interview and/or entrance exam held by the College Admission Committee
