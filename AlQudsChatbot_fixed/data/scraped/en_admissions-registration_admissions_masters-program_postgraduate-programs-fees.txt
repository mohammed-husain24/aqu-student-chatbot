Tuition Fee Payment Procedure
Procedure for Paying Tuition Fees via Banks or Electronic Payment Methods (For New Students Only)
PAYMENT DIRECTLY THROUGH THE BANK
Payment Directly Through the Bank
The student makes the payment to Al-Quds University’s account by visiting the bank and providing the application number and full name. Then, a copy of the payment receipt should be sent via email.
admissionhelp@alquds.edu
PAYMENT VIA ELECTRONIC VISA CARD
Payment via Electronic Visa Card
After logging into the online application portal, in the acceptance letter alongside the amount due, you will find a link that directs you to the payment screen for Visa card transactions. The payment notification is received by us on the same day.
BANK TRANSFERS FROM OTHER BANKS OR OUTSIDE PALESTINE
Bank Transfers from Other Banks or Outside Palestine
You can pay the tuition fees by transferring the amount directly from your account, whether inside or outside the country, to the university’s account at Palestine Bank using the banking information below. You must provide the Admissions Department with the student’s details (application number and full name) as well as the transfer details displayed on the screen after completing the transaction (transfer number, issuing bank, amount, and transaction date) via email at
admissionhelp@alquds.edu
.
