Campus & Facilities
HARMONIOUS PLACE
Harmonious Place
The Al-Quds University campus is a harmonious blend of historic charm and modern functionality, providing a serene and inspiring setting for intellectual pursuits. As you stroll through our picturesque grounds, you’ll be captivated by the lush gardens, tree-lined walkways, and inviting plazas that create a sense of tranquility and connection to nature. Our commitment to sustainability is evident in the environmentally friendly features and green spaces that enhance our campus’s beauty and foster a sense of responsibility towards our planet.
Enjoy Our Campus
By exploring our campus through virtual tours, interactive maps, and photo galleries, prospective students can get a sense of the vibrant and nurturing atmosphere that characterizes life at Al-Quds University.
Experience Beyond Expectations​​
MODERN CLASSROOMS
Modern Classrooms
Our classrooms are equipped with the latest technology, including interactive whiteboards, advanced audio-visual systems, and high-speed internet access, to facilitate dynamic and engaging learning experiences.
RESEARCH LABS
Research Labs
Our cutting-edge research labs provide students with access to advanced equipment and resources, fostering an environment of discovery and innovation across a wide range of disciplines, from natural sciences to engineering and humanities.
LIBRARY
Library
The Al-Quds University library is a treasure trove of knowledge, housing extensive collections of books, journals, and digital resources. The library features quiet study areas, group study rooms, computer stations, and a dedicated staff to assist students in their research and academic pursuits.
RECREATIONAL FACILITIES
Recreational Facilities
Our campus offers a variety of recreational facilities to promote health and well-being among our students. These include a modern fitness center, sports fields, and performance spaces for artistic expression and cultural events.
INCUBATOR & INNOVATION HUB
Incubator & Innovation Hub
The university's incubator is a vibrant center for entrepreneurship and innovation, providing students with the resources, mentorship, and support needed to develop their ideas into successful startups while still pursuing their studies.
DINING AND CAFETERIA
Dining and Cafeteria
Our dining facilities offer a wide range of delicious and nutritious meal options, catering to diverse tastes and dietary preferences. Students can enjoy meals in comfortable dining areas, cozy cafes, or take advantage of outdoor seating to enjoy their meals al fresco.
COMMON SPACES
Common Spaces
The campus features numerous inviting common spaces, such as lounges, study areas, and outdoor seating areas, where students can relax, socialize, and collaborate with their peers in a comfortable and welcoming environment.
