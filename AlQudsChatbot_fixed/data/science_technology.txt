Faculty of Science and Technology
-----------------------------

The College of Science and Technology consists of Science and Technology departments including Chemistry & Chemical Technology, Computer Science, Information Technology (Dual Studies), Mathematics, Physics, Life Sciences. :contentReference[oaicite:15]{index=15}

It is located on the Abu Dis Campus. The curriculum emphasizes fundamentals in the first years, then specialized courses, and culminates in research/design in the final year. :contentReference[oaicite:16]{index=16}
