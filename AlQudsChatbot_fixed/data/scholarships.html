Financial Aid & Scholarships

At Al‑Quds University, we aim to ensure that cost is not a barrier to education.

How the Unit Works

The Financial Aid Unit reviews applications, catalogs programs, verifies info, and coordinates with donors. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

Eligibility

  • Complete at least 12 credit hours
  • Be enrolled as a regular student
  • Register for 12+ credit hours per semester
  • Not have academic or disciplinary warnings
  • Meet additional donor criteria

Application

Submit a hard copy of the application form with supporting documents. Students can also benefit from external scholarships. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

External Scholarships

The university links to external scholarship providers such as Fulbright, Erasmus, the Open Society Foundations, etc. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}
